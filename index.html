Engine Services

Engine Repair

Oil Changes

Quick and thorough oil changes using quality motor oil and filters to keep your engine running smoothly.

Tune-Ups

Complete engine tune-ups including spark plug replacement, air filter service, and performance optimization.

Cooling System Service

Fuel System Service

Brake Services

Brake Inspection

Brake Pad Replacement

Quality brake pad installation with rotor resurfacing or replacement as needed.

Brake Rotor Service

Rotor turning and replacement to ensure optimal braking performance.

Brake Fluid Flush

Complete brake fluid replacement to maintain hydraulic system integrity.

ABS System Repair

Anti-lock brake system diagnostics and repair for modern vehicles.

Emergency Brake Repair

Parking brake adjustment, cable replacement, and emergency brake system service.

Transmission Repair

Transmission Rebuild

Full transmission rebuild with quality parts and comprehensive warranty.

Clutch Replacement

Manual transmission clutch replacement including pressure plate and flywheel service.

Differential Service

Differential fluid change and repair for front, rear, and all-wheel drive vehicles.

About Si's Auto Repair

Our Story

Si's Auto Repair has been a trusted name in Salt Lake City automotive service for years. Founded on the principles of honesty, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service, we've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time.

What started as a small neighborhood garage has grown into a full-service auto repair facility, but we've never forgotten our roots. We still treat every customer like family and every vehicle like it's our own.

Our team of ASE-certified technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job, from simple oil changes to complex engine repairs. We're equipped with the latest diagnostic equipment and tools to service all makes and models, domestic and foreign.
